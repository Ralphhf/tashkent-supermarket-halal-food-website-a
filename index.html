About Us

Authentic Halal Excellence Since 2005

Tashkent Supermarket brings the finest halal meats, fresh produce, and specialty foods from Central Asia and beyond to the heart of Manhattan. Our knowledgeable staff and commitment to quality ensure you'll find authentic ingredients for traditional recipes alongside everyday essentials, all certified halal and competitively priced.
